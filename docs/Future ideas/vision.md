# LotSync — Product Vision

## What LotSync Is

LotSync is the real-time inventory intelligence layer between dealer management systems and the physical lot.

The entry product is **ESL (Electronic Shelf Label) management with live pricing display** — tags attached to vehicles that update automatically when price, status, or inventory data changes. This solves a real, immediate operational problem for dealers: aging inventory that goes days or weeks without accurate pricing displayed on the lot, accumulating floor plan interest, manufacturer incentive penalties, and depreciation loss the dealer never recovers.

The foundational value proposition is not convenience. It is **cost avoidance**. Every day a vehicle sits on the lot with a stale price is a day of preventable loss. LotSync closes that gap in real time, automatically.

## The Core Problem We Solve (Phase 1)

Dealers face compounding losses on aging inventory:

- **Floor plan interest** accrues daily on every unsold unit
- **Manufacturer incentive clawbacks** penalize stale, overpriced inventory
- **Depreciation** accelerates the longer a vehicle doesn't move
- **Manual price updates** are slow, inconsistent, and require physical labor

Dealers currently manage this with paper window stickers, whiteboard pricing, and periodic lot walks. When DMS prices change, the lot doesn't know until someone walks out and manually changes the sticker.

LotSync automates that sync. Price changes in the DMS propagate to the ESL tag on the vehicle within seconds.

## The Larger Vision

Once LotSync is the operational layer between the DMS and the physical lot — with real-time data flowing through every vehicle on every lot — the platform becomes the foundation for inventory intelligence:

**LotSync knows:**
- What every vehicle is priced at, right now
- How long it's been on the lot
- How its price compares to market
- What similar vehicles are selling for regionally
- What the dealer's sell-through rate is by make/model/trim

That data, at scale across hundreds of dealerships, becomes a proprietary asset no competitor can replicate quickly.

The long-term vision is a **dealer intelligence platform** — not just displaying data, but recommending decisions:

> "Lower the Bronco $750. It's 12% above market and generating 40% fewer leads than comparable units."

> "You've sold 14 F-150s in 45 days and have 3 left. Consider acquiring more at auction this week."

> "Toyota hybrid demand is up 18% in your region. Competitor supply is thin."

The ESL platform is the right first product because it generates the data pipeline that makes everything else possible. But the starter product stands on its own — it delivers measurable ROI before any AI features are built.
